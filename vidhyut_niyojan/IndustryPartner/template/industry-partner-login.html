Login



When the industry partners completes the registration process in the portal their details will be verified by the admin. Once the verification process will complete industry partner can log in to the portal and publish job posts according to their requirements. After that, they will get hiring requests from the training partners or candidates.


Find Candidates



Once industry partners publish their post-training partners and the candidates will be notified of the job and its requirements Industry partners will get hiring requests on their published job-post by the training center or candidates who are interested in these jobs.


Interview Shedual

Whenever a training center or candidate sends hiring request to an industry partners they can see the candidate profile and their qualification. As per their requirement, they can accept the request and schedule interview.

Interview Result

After a successful interview industry partner can approve or reject the candidate as per their performance.
